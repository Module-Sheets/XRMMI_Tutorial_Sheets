MANUAL

NAVIGATION & VIEW

Mouse: Left Drag (Rotate) | Right Drag (Pan) | Scroll (Zoom)

Walk Mode: Click WALK, WASD to move, Mouse to look. ESC to exit.

Shortcuts: W Move E Rotate R Scale +/- Gizmo Size

CONSTRUCTION

  • Building: Drag on grid for Walls/Floors/Fences.
  • Shapes: Click to spawn primitives, pyramids, or arches.
  • Portals: Click walls to cut Doors or Windows.
  • Mirror: Toggle ON to create symmetrical copies automatically.
  • Tape Measure: Click Start, then End point to measure distance.
  • Align: Use Center X/Z or Drop to Floor for precision.

WORLD & ADVANCED

  • City Gen: Instantly create a random cityscape.
  • 3D Text/Trees: Procedural generators for scene details.
  • Textures: Upload custom images or use Procedural (Grid/Noise).
  • Lighting: Place physical Lamps and adjust Sky/Fog color.
  • CRT Mode: Toggle scanlines for that 90s monitor feel.

DEFORMATION

HOW TO USE:
1. Select Object.
2. Select Tool (e.g., Twist).
3. Hold SHIFT (or toggle on-screen SHIFT button).
4. Click & Drag horizontally on the object.
  • Warping: Twist, Twirl, Bend, Taper, Inflate.
  • Face/Verts: Face Drag (move surface), Magnet (soft pull).
  • Geometry: Extrude (pull face), Bevel (chamfer edges).
  • Random: Jitter (noise), Quantize (step/blocky look).

RETRO FX (INSTANT)

  • Atomizer: Convert edges to pipes and verts to spheres.
  • Greeble: Add random technological surface details.
  • Explode: Detach and push all faces outward.
  • Distortion: Melt (droop), Wave (sin), Ripple (radial).
  • Polygon Count: Simplify (Low-Poly) or Tessellate (Hi-Poly).
System: Snapshot (Camera), Save/Load (JSON), Export GLTF VR: Left Stick (Move) | Right Stick (Turn/Fly)
