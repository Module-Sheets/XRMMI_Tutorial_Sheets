Welcome to XR Multimodal Interaction

This tutorial series will guide you through the fundamentals of Extended Reality (XR) and multimodal interaction design. Each tutorial builds upon the previous one to provide comprehensive understanding of XR development using OpenXR Protocol and XRIT (XR Interaction Toolkit) in Unity.

Tip: Hover over each tutorial in the menu above to see more details, or click on any tutorial to get started.
